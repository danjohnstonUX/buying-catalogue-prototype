Nominate an organisation to create orders on your behalf

Get support procuring IT systems from the Buying Catalogue.

You can nominate an organisation registered on the Buying Catalogue to create orders on your behalf using our online form. You’ll need to provide the name of the organisation you're nominating and its ODS code if you know it.

We’ll send you an email to confirm this has been set up and then registered users from that organisation will be able to create orders for you.

The Buying Catalogue Team aims to respond to nomination requests within 5 working days.

If for any reason the button does not work, you can send your details to buying.catalogue@nhs.net with the subject line: Buying Catalogue nomination request.
