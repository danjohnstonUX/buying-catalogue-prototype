AccuRx features

  • Patient Triage is an online consultation solution to make communication with patients easier
  • Video consultations in two clicks without needing to download an app or create an account
  • SMS a patient with personalised messages saved instantly to the medical record
  • Patient questionnaires (such as for QOF) with >60% response rate (based on current usage)
  • Patient responses (including attaching photos/documents)
  • Send documents via SMS to patients
  • 100+ pre-written message templates or create custom templates for individuals or your organisation
  • Send a series of scheduled messages
  • SMS delivery receipts
  • Track practice usage
