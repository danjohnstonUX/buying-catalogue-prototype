Orders with a value less than £40k

For orders under £40k, you can execute a Direct Award procedure. This means you’re free to choose to procure any Catalogue Solution or service on the Buying Catalogue.

Your contract value should include everything you intend to spend.

It would not, for example, be acceptable to execute a number of Direct Awards to the same supplier to avoid carrying out a competition.

You’ll not be ready to place an order of this value until you’ve browsed the Buying Catalogue and identified what you want.
