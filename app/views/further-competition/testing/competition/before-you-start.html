Before you create a competition

Before you create a competition you should have:

  • used filters that match the requirements identified in your business case to create a shortlist
  • saved your filters
  • checked if any price has been agreed with the supplier other than the list price
  • identified the Service Recipients and the volume you want so you know the approximate cost of your order
  • decided if you want to evaluate solutions based on price only or price and non-price elements
  • changed to a direct award if your filters returned only 1 solution
