Welcome to browse Solutions

The Buying Catalogues provides you with all the Solutions and information you need to help you make an informed decision when procuring for you sites.

There are some key terms you need to understanding when using the Buying Catalogue:

  • Catalogue Solution = a product or service on the Buying Catalogue.
  • Capabilities = express a business need, for example, 'Recording Consultations' describes the need for General Practice staff to be able to make a digital record of any consultation with a patient.
  • Epics = the functions that make up the Capability, for example, under ‘Recording Consultations’ the Epics are: ‘record Consultation information’ and ‘manage Consultation form templates’. There are two types of Epic – Must Epics which must be met, and May Epics which are optional
  • Acceptance Criteria = describe the requirements of an Epic and are used to assess whether an Epic has been met
