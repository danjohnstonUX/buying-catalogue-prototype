Buying Catalogue

The NHS Digital Buying Catalogue is an online marketplace where you can purchase clinical information systems that meet digital needs in health and social care.

All the listed products – known as Catalogue Solutions – have been assessed against a set of Capabilities defined by the NHS and a set of Standards defined by NHS Digital.
