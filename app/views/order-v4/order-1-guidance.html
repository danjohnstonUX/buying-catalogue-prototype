Before you start an order

Important: GP IT Futures framework expired

Lot 1 of the GP IT Futures framework expired on 31 March 2023. This means you can no longer carry out new procurements of solutions and services offered under that framework.

Continuity contracts have been put in place to ensure that you continue to have access to purchased solutions and services between 1 April 2023 and the go live date of the replacement, which is scheduled for summer 2023.

You’ll still be able to use the Buying Catalogue to browse and procure solutions and services from the Digital First Online Consultation and Video Consultation (DFOCVC) framework.

However, orders placed under the GP IT Futures framework will not be completed until the replacement framework is live.

Before you start your order, you should know:

  • the approximate value of your order
  • what solutions and services you want to order
  • how you’re going to pay for your order
  • the approximate start date of your contract
  • the initial period as agreed with the supplier
  • the duration of the contract (maximum term)
  • which practices will be receiving the Catalogue Solutions and services being ordered
  • the quantity of Additional and Associated Services you want if you’re ordering any
  • contact details for the supplier of the Catalogue Solution and any services you’re ordering
  • if any price has been agreed with the supplier other than the list price
