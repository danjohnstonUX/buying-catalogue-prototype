Orders with a value between £40k and £250k

You must execute an On-Catalogue Competition procedure. This means you must carry out a competition of similar Catalogue Solutions using information available on the Buying Catalogue.

Score Catalogue Solutions based on how they perform against certain criteria, for example, price, implementation timescales or service levels. You’ll need to keep evidence of how you ended up selecting a particular solution.

You’ll not be ready to place an order of this value until this procedure has been carried out.
