About the Buying Catalogue

The NHS Digital Buying Catalogue is an online marketplace where you can purchase clinical information systems that meet digital needs in health and social care.

All the listed products – known as Catalogue Solutions – have been assessed against a set of Capabilities defined by the NHS and a set of Standards defined by NHS Digital.

The functionality of the Buying Catalogue will be enhanced and the number of Catalogue Solutions available will increase over time.

Frameworks

These are the frameworks currently available to procure from using the Buying Catalogue.

  • Tech Innovation framework

    Supports the delivery of cloud-based, innovative solutions in to the GP marketplace.

  • GP IT Futures framework

    Establishes a framework between NHS Digital and suppliers providing IT services to GPs.

  • DFOCVC framework

    Provides a single route for commissioners to buy nationally assured online consultation and video consultation solutions.
