Before you start an order

Before you start your order, you should know:

  • the approximate value of your order
  • what solutions and services you want to order
  • how you’re going to pay for your order
  • the approximate start date of your contract
  • the initial period as agreed with the supplier
  • the duration of the contract (maximum term)
  • which practices will be receiving the Catalogue Solutions and services being ordered
  • the quantity of Additional and Associated Services you want if you’re ordering any
  • contact details for the supplier of the Catalogue Solution and any services you’re ordering
  • if any price has been agreed with the supplier other than the list price
